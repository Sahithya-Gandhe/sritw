Welcome to SRITW

A Premier Institution for Women's Engineering Education

Established in 2009, Sumathi Reddy Institute of Technology for Women (SRITW) is committed to providing a world-class education and fostering innovation among young women. Our mission is to empower the next generation of female leaders in science and technology.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we provide a nurturing environment where students can achieve their full potential. Our programs are NBA and NAAC accredited, ensuring the highest standards of academic excellence.
